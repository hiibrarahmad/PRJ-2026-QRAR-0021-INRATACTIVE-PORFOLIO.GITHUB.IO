How to use

1. Scan the QR

Open the AR experience in your mobile browser.

2. Enter AR

Tap Enter AR. If you are in marker mode, point the camera at the QR marker.

3. Explore repos

Cycle through projects and open GitHub links.

Compatibility

WebXR AR works best on Android Chrome. iPhone users are automatically routed to Marker Mode for camera access. If AR is not supported, use Marker Mode.

Marker Mode shows the portfolio only while the QR marker is visible in the camera.
